DM_KARL

for a level editing course, we had to create a map for the 2-3 players for the competitive shooter Unreal Tournament, made by Epic Games. We started by making a level that had to flow well for movement and had to encourage good gameplay. Later on, we had to decorate this level using modular props and decorations using trim materials to make it possible for 1 student to finish an entire level.

this was also my first experience with the unreal material editor to make animated materials and tileable materials.
